Lama Film

 
 

SYNOPSIS

A child is playing in suburb lanes while an African young woman prostitutes herself near there. To escape from a Police round up, the young prostitute finds a shelter in those lanes. During a sudden summer downpour, she meets the child who will save her from a Police arrest.

 

 

BIOGRAPHY and FILM BIOGRAPHY OF THE AUTHOR

Giuseppe K. Miglietta was born on the 13th July 1975 in Foggia. In 2001 he got a degree in “Scienze della comunicazione” in Università degli Studi of Salerno with a thesis about “Theory and technique of the cinematographic language”. In 2003 he obtained a diploma in direction by the N.U.C.T., New University of Cinema and Television, in Rome. From 2003 he was the assistant director in some TV movies as “Paolo Borsellino” by Gianluca Maria Tavarelli, “Distretto di Polizia”, “Nassiriya” by Michele Soavi. In 2003, he wrote and directed his first short film “La stanza di Caronte” that was presented in several national and international festivals such as “Bellaria film festival 2004” and “Med film festival 2004”.
